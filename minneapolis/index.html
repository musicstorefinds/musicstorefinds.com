⭐ Featured Store
Electric Fetus
📍 Cedar-Riverside
💿 Minneapolis Legend Since 1968

Electric Fetus is one of the great American independent record stores — a Minneapolis institution since 1968 that has survived every disruption the music industry has faced by remaining genuinely essential to its community. The store carries an extraordinary selection of new and used vinyl, CDs, and music-adjacent merchandise spanning every genre.

Prince was a regular customer. Bob Dylan grew up in the region. The Replacements formed a few miles away. Electric Fetus has served the musical community that produced all of them, and it continues to serve Minneapolis musicians, music lovers, and record collectors with the same passion that has defined it for over 55 years.

Shopping at Electric Fetus is one of the great independent record store experiences in America. The staff knowledge is exceptional, the selection is remarkable, and the store's role in Minneapolis musical culture is irreplaceable. If you're visiting Minneapolis and you love music, this is the first stop.

Best for: Record collectors, music lovers, and anyone wanting to experience Minneapolis's musical legacy.

⭐ Featured Store
Groth Music
📍 Bloomington
🎸 The Midwest's Most Comprehensive Music Store

Groth Music in Bloomington has built a reputation as one of the most comprehensive independent music stores in the Midwest. The store carries an extraordinary range of instruments across every category — guitars, basses, drums, keyboards, band and orchestra instruments, DJ gear, and recording equipment — alongside a serious repair operation and active music school.

What sets Groth apart from a typical full-service store is the depth of inventory within each category. This isn't a shop where guitars are an afterthought while drums get the prime floor space. Every instrument category is taken seriously, staffed by specialists, and stocked with real depth.

For Minneapolis and Twin Cities musicians who want a genuine alternative to the big-box chains, Groth Music is the answer — a shop with the selection and expertise of a specialist combined with the breadth of a full-service store.

Why Musicians Love It
  • One of the most comprehensive independent music stores in the Midwest
  • Every instrument category staffed by genuine specialists with real depth of inventory
  • Active repair shop and music school serving the Twin Cities community
  • A genuine full-service alternative to the big-box chains
Best for: Twin Cities musicians of all levels seeking any instrument category with genuine expertise.
