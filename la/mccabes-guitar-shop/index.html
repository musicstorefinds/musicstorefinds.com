McCabe's Guitar Shop

📍 3101 Pico Blvd, Santa Monica, CA 90405
Visit Website → Get Directions
McCabe's Guitar Shop is one of the most storied instrument stores in the country. Founded in 1958, it specializes in acoustic and folk instruments from ouds and sitars to hammered dulcimers, fiddles, and ethnic percussion. Since 1969 the intimate 150-seat back room has hosted thousands of concerts with artists like Jackson Browne, Tom Waits, and Ry Cooder.
