🗺 Plan Your Music Store Tour
The best areas for music shopping in Los Angeles — organized so you can hit multiple stores in one trip.
📍 Santa Monica & Westside
Truetone Music and McCabe's Guitar Shop anchor this strip — perfect for acoustic and boutique electric gear.
📍 Hollywood & Burbank
Pro Drum Shop, Perfect Circuit, and Vintage King cluster here — essential for studio musicians and producers.
📍 Downtown LA & Silver Lake
Caveman Vintage, International House of Music, and Old Style Guitar Shop make for a great East Side day trip.
📍 San Fernando Valley
Norman's Rare Guitars and Angel City Guitars are worth the drive for serious players.
