About

Truetone Music has been a destination for serious players for over 30 years. Their showroom is packed with new, used, and vintage guitars, boutique amps, and effects pedals from top brands like Martin, Gibson, Fender, and rare boutique manufacturers. Every instrument is professionally inspected and set up before it hits the floor. They also offer lessons and expert repairs.
