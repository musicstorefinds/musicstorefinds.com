🗺 Plan Your Music Store Tour
The best areas for music shopping in Seattle — organized so you can hit multiple stores in one trip.
📍 Fremont
Dusty Strings is the anchor of Fremont's creative community — essential for folk and acoustic musicians.
📍 Ballard
Bop Street Records, Sonic Boom, and Thunder Road Guitars make Ballard Seattle's richest music retail neighborhood.
📍 Pioneer Square & Georgetown
Emerald City Guitars and Georgetown Music Store serve players seeking vintage and used gear downtown.
📍 West Seattle & Capitol Hill
Easy Street Records in West Seattle and Sub Pop Megamart near Capitol Hill round out the city's record store scene.
