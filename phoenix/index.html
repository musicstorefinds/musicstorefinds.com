Best Music Stores
in Phoenix, Arizona

Phoenix's music scene has grown significantly with the city's population explosion — Arizona now has a thriving independent music community anchored in neighborhoods like Roosevelt Row and Tempe's Mill Avenue district. The music stores that serve this community range from the vintage specialists of Scottsdale to the full-service shops that serve Phoenix's enormous population.

Stinkweeds Records in central Phoenix is one of the Southwest's most beloved independent record stores, carrying a remarkable range of new and used vinyl alongside new releases from independent labels. The store has served as a community hub for Phoenix's alternative music scene for over 30 years.

Phoenix's desert climate creates unique conditions for instruments — the dry air affects wood instruments significantly, and the best local shops know how to advise customers on humidity control and maintenance. It's a detail that separates the genuine Phoenix music specialists from the generic chains.

⭐ Featured Store
Stinkweeds Records
📍 Central Phoenix
💿 Phoenix's Essential Independent Record Store

Stinkweeds Records has been the heart of Phoenix's independent music community since 1987. The store carries an extraordinary range of new releases from independent labels alongside a well-curated used vinyl section — and has been an essential resource for Phoenix music lovers through every shift the industry has undergone.

The shop's location in Central Phoenix puts it at the heart of Roosevelt Row — Phoenix's arts district and the anchor of the city's creative community. Stinkweeds is more than a record store; it's a community gathering place, an event space, and a cultural institution that has shaped the taste and community of Phoenix's music scene for 35+ years.

For anyone visiting Phoenix who loves music, Stinkweeds Records is a required stop.

Record Parlour Phoenix
📍 Phoenix
💿 Phoenix Vinyl Destination

Record Parlour has established itself as one of Phoenix's best sources for used vinyl — a carefully curated shop that carries a broad range of records at fair prices, with strong depth in the rock, soul, and jazz genres that Phoenix collectors seek most.

The shop's approach to curation sets it apart from a generic used record shop — every record in the store has been vetted and priced by people who genuinely understand what they're selling. That knowledge translates to a better shopping experience for serious collectors and casual browsers alike.

For Phoenix record hunters, Record Parlour is a reliable source for quality used vinyl at honest prices.

  • Every record vetted and priced by genuine music knowledge
