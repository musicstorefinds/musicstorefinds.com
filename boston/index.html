Best Music Stores
in Boston, Massachusetts

Boston's music scene is shaped by two forces: one of the highest concentrations of universities and music schools in the world — Berklee, NEC, MIT, Harvard — and a deep working-class music culture rooted in Irish traditional music, rock, and folk. The city's music stores serve both communities, from the professional Berklee student seeking a specific piece of gear to the Irish session musician looking for a quality fiddle.

The city lost Daddy's Junky Music — a beloved New England institution — in 2011, which created a gap in the market that smaller independent shops have worked to fill. Wally's Music Shop in Quincy has become one of the most trusted instrument shops in the Boston area. In Your Ear Music in Jamaica Plain brings the eclectic energy of that neighborhood to its record selection.

Boston's music retail scene rewards exploration. The Berklee-adjacent shops on Massachusetts Avenue are dense with gear aimed at working students and professionals. Cambridge has its own ecosystem of record stores and instrument shops that serve the city's academic and creative communities.

Cambridge Music
📍 Cambridge
🎸 Greater Boston's Guitar Destination

Cambridge Music has established itself as one of the most respected independent music stores in the Greater Boston area. Located in Cambridge — home to Harvard, MIT, and a thriving creative community — the shop carries a thoughtful selection of guitars, basses, amplifiers, and effects alongside expert repair services that have earned the trust of Boston's professional musician community.

The Cambridge location is ideal — surrounded by universities, recording studios, and a dense population of working musicians who need quality instruments and reliable service. The staff's knowledge reflects this environment: deep, honest, and genuinely helpful.

For Boston musicians who want the depth of inventory and expertise you'd expect from a major city guitar shop, Cambridge Music consistently delivers.

⭐ Featured Store
In Your Ear Music
📍 Jamaica Plain
💿 Boston's Eclectic Record Shop

In Your Ear Music in Jamaica Plain is one of Boston's most beloved independent record stores — a shop that reflects the eclectic, community-oriented character of its Jamaica Plain neighborhood. The store carries a broad selection of used vinyl and CDs across all genres, with a particular strength in the kind of eclectic and hard-to-find music that the staff and community genuinely love.

Jamaica Plain has always been one of Boston's most creative neighborhoods, with a strong arts and music community that In Your Ear serves as a gathering place. The store has survived decades of industry disruption by building genuine community loyalty.

For Boston record collectors who've exhausted the obvious options, In Your Ear consistently offers interesting finds at fair prices.

Why Musicians Love It
  • Jamaica Plain's beloved independent record store and community institution
  • Broad selection of used vinyl and CDs with eclectic, well-curated inventory
  • Reflects the creative character of JP — open-minded, community-oriented
  • Fair prices and interesting finds for Boston record collectors
Best for: Record collectors and music lovers in Boston seeking eclectic used vinyl at fair prices.
