Louisiana Music Factory sits at the heart of New Orleans' live music corridor — the best place to find authentic Louisiana music on vinyl.
📍 Bywater
Webb's Bywater Music, Euclid Records, and Piety Street Recordings make the Bywater a destination for music lovers.
📍 Magazine Street & Uptown
Uptown Music Exchange and Uptown Music serve the Magazine Street corridor's musicians.
