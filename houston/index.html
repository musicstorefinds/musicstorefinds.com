Best Music Stores
in Houston, Texas

Houston's music scene reflects the city's enormous size and cultural diversity — a sprawling metropolis with a remarkable range of musical traditions from country and blues to tejano, zydeco, and hip-hop. The city that produced Beyoncé, Travis Scott, and ZZ Top has a music community that takes its instruments seriously.

Houston's music retail scene includes everything from the guitar specialists of the Montrose neighborhood to the full-service chains that serve the city's enormous suburban population. Cactus Records is one of the most beloved independent record stores in Texas, carrying an extraordinary range of vinyl that reflects Houston's diverse musical tastes.

The city's size means the best music stores can be spread across a significant distance, but Houston's distinct neighborhoods — Montrose, the Heights, Midtown — each have their own music retail character worth exploring.

⭐ Featured Store
Cactus Records
📍 Rice Village
💿 Houston's Beloved Record Store Since 1975

Cactus Records in Rice Village has been one of Houston's most beloved cultural institutions since 1975. The store carries a remarkable selection of new and used vinyl, CDs, and music-related merchandise spanning every genre — with particular strength in the Texas music traditions of country, blues, and tejano alongside rock, jazz, and soul.

The store has survived 50 years of music industry transformation by remaining genuinely essential to its community. Regular in-store performances and events have made Cactus Records a gathering place for Houston music lovers, not just a retail destination. The staff knowledge is exceptional, and the curation reflects decades of genuine music passion.

If you're in Houston and you love music, Cactus Records is required. It's one of the great independent record stores of Texas.

⭐ Featured Store
Sound Exchange Houston
📍 Montrose
🎸 Montrose Guitar & Gear Shop

Sound Exchange in Montrose is Houston's neighborhood music store for the city's most creative and music-dense community. Montrose has always been Houston's bohemian heart — the neighborhood where artists, musicians, and creative people of all kinds have clustered — and Sound Exchange serves that community with quality instruments and genuine music knowledge.

The shop carries guitars, basses, amplifiers, and accessories alongside a used and vintage selection that attracts Houston players looking for quality gear at fair prices. The Montrose location gives Sound Exchange a character that a suburban chain can never replicate.

For Houston musicians who want the authenticity of an independent neighborhood shop, Sound Exchange delivers.

Why Musicians Love It
  • Montrose's neighborhood music shop — at the heart of Houston's most creative community
  • Guitars, basses, amps, and accessories with used and vintage selection
  • Reflects Montrose's bohemian, music-loving character
  • The authentic independent alternative for Houston musicians
Best for: Houston musicians in and around Montrose seeking quality instruments and repairs.
