The best areas for music shopping in Austin — organized so you can hit multiple stores in one trip.
📍 South Lamar Corridor
South Austin Music, Austin Vintage Guitars, and End of an Ear cluster on and around S Lamar — the heart of Austin's music retail scene.
📍 North Lamar & Central
The new Waterloo Records is now at 1105 N Lamar — bigger and better than ever with a stage and bar.
📍 Guadalupe & University Area
Antone's Record Shop near UT serves Austin's student and blues music community.
📍 North Austin & Suburbs
Strait Music's north location and Guitar Sanctuary in McKinney serve the broader Austin metro.
