🗺 Plan Your Music Store Tour
The best areas for music shopping in Chicago — organized so you can hit multiple stores in one trip.
📍 Lincoln Square
Chicago Music Exchange and Old Town School Music Store make this neighborhood a must for guitarists and folk musicians.

Rock N Roll Vintage, Beaten Path Music, and Dusty Groove make this one of Chicago's richest music shopping corridors.
📍 Lakeview & Andersonville
Guitar Chicago, Evolution Music, and Russo Music serve Chicago's north side music community.
📍 The Loop & Near North
