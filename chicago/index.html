Best Music Stores
in Chicago

Chicago's music store scene is as deep and soulful as the city's musical legacy. From the blues clubs of the South Side to the indie rock venues of Wicker Park, Chicago has always been a city where musicians work, and its instrument shops reflect that. The city has produced some of the most distinctive music retail destinations in the country.

Chicago Music Exchange on North Clark Street has become one of the most respected guitar shops in the Midwest — a destination shop that draws serious players from across the region. Dusty Groove America has built a global reputation as one of the world's great used record stores, specializing in soul, jazz, and hip-hop vinyl that attracts collectors from every continent.

Whether you're looking for a vintage Stratocaster, a rare soul 45, a custom drum kit, or lessons from a working jazz musician, Chicago's music stores deliver. The city's DIY ethos means many of the best shops are independently owned, deeply knowledgeable, and genuinely invested in the local music community.

⭐ Featured Store
Chicago Music Exchange
📍 Lakeview
🎸 Chicago's Premier Guitar Destination

Chicago Music Exchange on North Clark Street has established itself as one of the most respected independent guitar shops in the Midwest. The floor is packed with new, used, and vintage guitars spanning every price point and style — from entry-level acoustics to museum-quality vintage electrics that would be at home in a collector's vault.

What sets CME apart is the combination of extraordinary inventory and genuine expertise. The staff are working musicians who know the instruments deeply, and the shop's repair and setup operation is considered among the best in Chicago. They also run a robust online presence that ships gear worldwide.

CME hosts regular in-store events, artist visits, and gear demos that make it a cultural hub as much as a retail destination. If you're a guitar player visiting Chicago, this is the first stop on your list.

  • One of the deepest guitar inventories in the Midwest — new, used, and vintage
  • Expert repair and setup shop staffed by working Chicago musicians
  • Regular in-store events and artist visits
  • Online store ships worldwide — a true destination shop
Best for: Guitar players of all levels seeking the widest selection in Chicago.

⭐ Featured Store
Dusty Groove America
📍 Wicker Park
💿 World-Famous Record Store

Dusty Groove America has built a global reputation over 25+ years as one of the world's great used record stores. Specializing in soul, jazz, funk, hip-hop, and Brazilian music, Dusty Groove attracts collectors from every continent — and their online store ships to music fans worldwide who can't make the trip to Wicker Park.

The physical store on North Milwaukee Avenue is a treasure hunter's paradise. Tens of thousands of used LPs and 45s organized with genuine curatorial care. The staff know their inventory with encyclopedic depth, and the selection is refreshed constantly as they buy collections from across the country.

Dusty Groove is as important to Chicago's cultural identity as any blues club or jazz venue. If you're a record collector and you visit Chicago without stopping here, you've made a serious mistake.

Why Musicians Love It
  • One of the world's most respected soul, jazz, funk, and hip-hop record stores
  • Tens of thousands of used LPs and 45s with expert curation
  • Online store ships worldwide — attracts collectors from every continent
  • Constantly refreshed inventory — no two visits are the same
Best for: Vinyl collectors, DJs, and music fans seeking rare soul, jazz, and hip-hop records.
