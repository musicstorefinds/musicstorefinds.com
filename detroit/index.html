Best Music Stores
in Detroit, Michigan

Detroit's musical legacy is unmatched in American history. Motown. Techno. MC5. The White Stripes. Aretha Franklin. The city that invented the sound of modern American music has a music store scene that honors that heritage with genuine depth and character.

Detroit's music retail scene has contracted with the city's economic challenges, but what survives is genuinely excellent. Huber and Breese in Royal Oak is one of the most trusted instrument shops in southeast Michigan. Found Sound in Ferndale has built a devoted following as one of the region's best used record shops. The city's techno heritage is reflected in several shops specializing in DJ gear and electronic music equipment.

Detroit musicians are resourceful and community-oriented — characteristics that show up in the city's independent music shops. These aren't flashy showrooms. They're working shops that serve working musicians, with the kind of no-nonsense expertise that the Detroit music community has always valued.

⭐ Featured Store
Huber and Breese Music
📍 Royal Oak
🎸 Southeast Michigan's Trusted Music Store

Huber and Breese Music in Royal Oak has been one of the most trusted instrument shops in Southeast Michigan for decades. The shop carries a solid selection of guitars, basses, amplifiers, and band instruments alongside expert repair services that have earned the loyalty of Detroit-area musicians.

Royal Oak is the ideal location for a shop serving the broader Detroit metro area — a community with a thriving arts scene and a dense population of working musicians who value substance over flash. Huber and Breese delivers exactly that: honest advice, quality instruments, and reliable repair work.

In a region where the independent music retail landscape has thinned considerably, Huber and Breese stands as an anchor — a shop that Detroit-area musicians can depend on.

Why Musicians Love It
  • One of Southeast Michigan's most trusted independent music stores
  • Expert repair shop serving Detroit-area professionals and students
  • Solid selection of guitars, basses, amps, and band instruments
  • Royal Oak location serves the broad Detroit metro musician community
Best for: Detroit-area musicians seeking quality instruments and reliable repair service.

⭐ Featured Store
Found Sound
📍 Ferndale
💿 Detroit's Beloved Record Shop

Found Sound in Ferndale has built a devoted following as one of the best used record shops in the Detroit metro area. The store carries a carefully curated selection of vinyl across all genres, with particular strength in the Detroit-relevant genres of soul, funk, R&B, techno, and rock.

Ferndale is a natural home for a shop like this — one of Detroit's most creative and community-oriented suburbs, with a thriving arts scene and a population of music lovers who take their vinyl seriously. Found Sound reflects the neighborhood's character: unpretentious, knowledgeable, and genuinely passionate about music.

For Detroit record collectors, Found Sound is an essential stop — the kind of shop where you always find something you weren't looking for but absolutely needed.

Why Musicians Love It
  • One of the best used record shops in the Detroit metro area
  • Curated selection strong in soul, funk, R&B, techno, and Detroit rock
  • Ferndale neighborhood shop — unpretentious and genuinely passionate
  • The kind of shop where you always leave with something unexpected
Best for: Detroit record collectors, DJs, and music lovers seeking used vinyl across all genres.
