Best Music Stores
in Denver, Colorado

Denver's music scene has exploded alongside the city's broader growth — but the music store scene has maintained the character of a city that takes its music seriously. From the folk and bluegrass specialists of the Capitol Hill neighborhood to the full-service shops of Aurora and Lakewood, Denver's independent music retailers serve one of the most musically passionate communities in the Mountain West.

Swallow Hill Music is the crown jewel of Denver's music community — a nonprofit music school and concert venue that has been at the heart of Denver's folk, bluegrass, and Americana scene for over 45 years. The Rocky Mountain Fiddle Shop is one of the most specialized instrument shops anywhere in the region, serving the bluegrass and old-time music community that has always been strong in Colorado.

Denver's outdoor culture shapes its music — bluegrass festivals, folk concerts, and acoustic music are deeply embedded in the city's identity. The best music stores reflect that, with a strong emphasis on acoustic instruments, folk music, and the kind of community-oriented retail that makes musicians feel at home.

⭐ Featured Store
Swallow Hill Music
📍 South Denver
🎸 Denver's Folk Music Institution Since 1979

Swallow Hill Music is the heart of Denver's folk, bluegrass, and Americana community — a nonprofit music school, concert venue, and community organization that has been serving Colorado musicians since 1979. With over 800 classes per year and one of the most active concert series in the city, Swallow Hill is as much a cultural institution as a music business.

The organization's retail arm carries a thoughtful selection of acoustic instruments — particularly guitars, banjos, ukuleles, and folk instruments — alongside the sheet music, accessories, and instrument rentals that serve Swallow Hill's enormous student community. The staff's knowledge of acoustic and folk instruments is exceptional.

Swallow Hill's concerts have introduced Denver audiences to hundreds of folk, bluegrass, and Americana artists over four decades. Artists who have performed at Swallow Hill range from local Colorado musicians to Grammy-winning national acts. It is, simply, one of the best music organizations in the Mountain West.

Why Musicians Love It
  • Denver's folk and acoustic music institution since 1979 — 45+ years of community service
  • Over 800 music classes per year across guitar, banjo, ukulele, fiddle, and more
  • Active concert series featuring local and national folk and Americana artists
  • Nonprofit mission means genuine community investment — not just retail
Best for: Folk, bluegrass, and acoustic music players seeking lessons, instruments, and community in Denver.

⭐ Featured Store
Rocky Mountain Fiddle Shop
📍 Denver
🎻 Colorado's Fiddle and Strings Specialist

Rocky Mountain Fiddle Shop is one of the most specialized instrument shops in the Mountain West — a dedicated violin and fiddle specialist that serves both classical players and the region's strong bluegrass and old-time music community. Colorado's mountain culture has always had a deep connection to fiddle music, and the Rocky Mountain Fiddle Shop is the shop that serves that community.

The shop carries a comprehensive range of violins, violas, cellos, and basses alongside specialized fiddle accessories, strings, rosin, and cases. Their repair and restoration work is trusted by professional orchestral musicians and touring bluegrass fiddlers alike.

This is the kind of specialist shop that a city needs but often lacks — deeply focused, genuinely knowledgeable, and irreplaceable for the community it serves.

Why Musicians Love It
  • Colorado's dedicated violin, viola, cello, and fiddle specialist
  • Serves both classical musicians and the region's bluegrass and old-time community
  • Expert repair and restoration trusted by professional orchestral musicians
  • Comprehensive inventory of strings, bows, accessories, and cases
Best for: Fiddle and orchestral string players in Denver and across the Mountain West.
