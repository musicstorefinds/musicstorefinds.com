Best Music Stores
in Portland, Oregon

Portland's music store scene reflects the city's broader character — fiercely independent, deeply community-oriented, and allergic to anything corporate. The city's thriving DIY music culture has sustained a remarkable collection of independent instrument shops, record stores, and music-related businesses that would struggle to survive in most American cities.

Mississippi Avenue in North Portland has become the city's music retail hub, with Reverb Guitars and several other shops clustered in the neighborhood's converted bungalows and storefronts. Mississippi Records — not affiliated with the Mississippi Avenue neighborhood — is one of the most respected reissue labels and record stores in the country, operating out of a tiny North Portland shop.

Portland musicians tend to value authenticity and community over flashiness, and the city's music stores reflect that. You won't find many high-pressure sales floors here — instead, you'll find shops run by passionate musicians who genuinely care about the instruments they sell and the players they serve.

⭐ Featured Store
Reverb Guitars
📍 Mississippi Avenue
🎸 Portland's Premier Guitar Shop

Reverb Guitars on Mississippi Avenue is Portland's most respected independent guitar shop — a focused, expertly curated store that carries new, used, and vintage guitars with the kind of depth you'd expect from a shop run by serious players.

The shop has built a strong reputation among Portland's professional musician community for honest advice, fair prices, and expert repair work. The staff are working musicians who know their instruments and will give you a straight answer about what's worth playing and what isn't.

Mississippi Avenue is the ideal setting for a shop like this — one of Portland's most walkable and community-oriented commercial strips, with cafes, bookstores, and independent businesses that make the neighborhood feel like a genuine place rather than a retail corridor.

Why Musicians Love It
  • Portland's most respected independent guitar shop on Mississippi Avenue
  • Expert repair and setup from staff who are working Portland musicians
  • Honest advice and fair prices — no high-pressure sales
  • New, used, and vintage guitars in a carefully curated selection
Best for: Portland guitar players of all levels seeking quality instruments and honest advice.

⭐ Featured Store
Mississippi Records
📍 North Portland
💿 World-Renowned Reissue Label & Record Store

Mississippi Records is one of the most respected names in the independent record world — simultaneously a record store, a reissue label, and an institution. Operating out of a small North Portland shop, Mississippi has spent 20+ years releasing carefully researched reissues of rare folk, blues, gospel, and world music recordings that would otherwise be lost to history.

The store itself is a treasure — filled with new and used records, with a particular focus on the genres Mississippi champions through its label: pre-war blues, gospel, African music, Indonesian psych, and other musical traditions that most record stores don't cover at all. The owner's taste and knowledge are extraordinary.

Mississippi Records is the kind of place that reminds you why physical record stores matter. You cannot replicate the experience of flipping through their bins and discovering something that reframes your understanding of what music can be.

Why Musicians Love It
  • World-renowned reissue label AND record store in one tiny North Portland shop
  • 20+ years releasing rare folk, blues, gospel, and world music reissues
  • Deep collection of records in genres most stores don't carry at all
  • An essential destination for any serious record collector visiting Portland
Best for: Record collectors, music researchers, and anyone interested in the deep roots of American and world music.
